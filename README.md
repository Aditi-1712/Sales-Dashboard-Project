# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Business Problem

A retail company requires better visibility into its sales performance across products, customers, and geographic regions. Existing reports provide raw transaction data but do not offer actionable insights for decision-makers. The objective of this project is to develop an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, and make data-driven business decisions.

## Dataset

The dataset contains historical retail sales transactions, including:

* Order information
* Product categories and subcategories
* Customer details
* Geographic locations
* Sales revenue
* Profit metrics
* Order dates

The data was cleaned, transformed, and modeled to support interactive reporting and analysis in Power BI.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Modeling
* Data Cleaning and Transformation
* Business Intelligence Reporting

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

* Total Sales Revenue
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Product Category
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Top Performing Products
* Customer Performance Analysis
* Profitability Analysis

## Dashboard Features

* Executive summary page with key business metrics
* Interactive filtering by region, category, and time period
* Sales trend analysis over time
* Product performance comparison
* Regional performance visualization
* Customer segmentation insights
* Profitability tracking

## Key Insights

### 1. Regional Performance Differences

Sales performance varies significantly across regions, indicating opportunities to replicate successful strategies from high-performing regions and address challenges in lower-performing markets.

### 2. Product Category Contribution

A small number of product categories generate a significant portion of total revenue, suggesting that inventory and marketing efforts should prioritize these high-performing categories.

### 3. Seasonal Sales Trends

Sales activity fluctuates throughout the year, revealing seasonal patterns that can support more effective forecasting and resource planning.

### 4. Profitability Variations

Some products generate strong sales volumes but contribute relatively lower profit margins, highlighting opportunities to improve pricing strategies and profitability.

### 5. Customer Value Distribution

A relatively small segment of customers contributes a large share of overall revenue, demonstrating the importance of customer retention and relationship management strategies.

## Recommendations

### Increase Focus on High-Performing Products

Allocate additional marketing resources and inventory support toward products that consistently generate high revenue and profit.

### Improve Underperforming Regions

Investigate operational, pricing, and marketing factors affecting low-performing regions and develop targeted improvement initiatives.

### Strengthen Customer Retention

Implement loyalty programs and personalized engagement strategies for high-value customers to maximize lifetime value.

### Enhance Forecasting Capabilities

Use historical sales trends to improve demand forecasting and inventory management.

### Optimize Product Portfolio

Review products with low profitability and evaluate opportunities for pricing adjustments, supplier negotiations, or discontinuation.

## Project Outcome

This project demonstrates the use of business intelligence tools to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights. The dashboard provides stakeholders with a centralized view of organizational performance and supports data-driven decision-making.
